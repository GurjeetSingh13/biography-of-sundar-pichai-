Sundar Pichai

"Wear your failure as a badge of honor."

Sundar Pichai is a prominent technology executive and the CEO of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Here is a detailed overview of his life and career:

Early Life

  • Full Name: Pichai Sundararajan
  • Date of Birth: June 10, 1972
  • Place of Birth:Madurai, Tamil Nadu, India
  • Parents His father, Regunatha Pichai, was an electrical engineer, and his mother, Lakshmi Pichai, was a stenographer.

Education

  • Undergraduate: Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) in Metallurgical Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur. He was awarded a Silver Medal for his academic performance.
  • Graduate: Master of Science (M.S.) in Material Sciences and Engineering from Stanford University.
  • MBA:Master of Business Administration (MBA) from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania,where he was named a Siebel Scholar and a Palmer Scholar.

Career

  • Early Career

  • Pichai began his career as a materials engineer. Before joining Google, he worked at Applied Materials and in management consulting at McKinsey & Company.

  • Google:

  • Joining Google: Pichai joined Google in 2004.

  • Product Management and Development: He initially worked on the Google Toolbar and later led the development of Google Chrome, which became the world's most popular web browser.
  • Other Key Projects:He was also involved in the development of other important products like Google Drive,Gmail, and Google Maps.

Personal Life

  • Family: Pichai is married to Anjali Pichai, and they have two children.
  • Citizenship: He holds both Indian and American citizenship.
Sundar Pichai's journey from a modest upbringing in India to becoming the CEO of one of the world's most influential tech companies is a testament to his vision, perseverance, and leadership.
